TikTok
Short-Form Video

TikTok is a short-form video platform powered by one of the world's most sophisticated AI recommendation engines. Its "For You Page" (FYP) uses machine learning to analyse every interaction — watch time, replays, shares — delivering hyper-personalised content within seconds of joining. Videos range from 15 seconds to 10 minutes, spanning music, comedy, education, and niche subcultures.

Facebook
Social Network

Facebook is the world's largest social network, built on the concept of connecting real people with their authentic identities. It powers personal profiles, business pages, groups, events, Marketplace, and Reels. Its News Feed algorithm prioritises "meaningful social interactions" — posts from friends, family, and groups you engage with most. Facebook also underpins a vast advertising ecosystem reaching 2+ billion people.

Instagram
Visual Platform

Instagram is a visual-first platform owned by Meta, built around photos, Reels, Stories, and live video. Its Explore page surfaces content from accounts you don't follow using interest-based AI. Instagram is the dominant platform for influencer marketing, fashion, travel, food, and lifestyle content. Reels — Instagram's short-form video answer to TikTok — now drives the majority of the platform's reach and engagement.

WhatsApp
Encrypted Messaging

WhatsApp is the world's leading private messaging app, offering end-to-end encrypted text, voice, video, and document sharing for individuals and groups of up to 1,024 members. Owned by Meta, it operates on a phone-number identity system and requires no username. It is the primary communication tool for billions in South Asia, Latin America, Africa, and Europe. WhatsApp Business adds commerce and customer service capabilities.

Snapchat
Ephemeral Media

Snapchat pioneered the concept of ephemeral content — photos and videos that disappear after viewing. It introduced Stories (now copied by every major platform), AR Lenses, and Snap Map. Snapchat's core audience skews younger (13–24), and its AI features, including My AI chatbot, are deeply integrated. The platform's unique "Streak" mechanic drives daily engagement through social accountability.

X (Twitter)
Public Discourse

X, formerly Twitter, is the world's primary public conversation platform — a real-time text-first network where breaking news, political discourse, sports commentary, and cultural moments unfold live. Its chronological and algorithmic feeds surface posts from followed accounts and trending topics. Under Elon Musk, X has introduced premium subscriptions (X Premium), expanded post lengths, and introduced Community Notes for collaborative fact-checking.

Telegram
Secure Messaging & Channels

Telegram is a cloud-based messaging platform that combines private chats, large group conversations (up to 200,000 members), and broadcast Channels with no subscriber limit. It is renowned for its speed, file-sharing capabilities (up to 2GB per file), bot ecosystem, and optional Secret Chats with end-to-end encryption. Telegram has become the platform of choice for communities, newsletters, and decentralised movements worldwide.
